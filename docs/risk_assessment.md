# Risk Assessment

## Identified Risks

### 1. Email Exposure
Predictable email formats increase phishing risk.

### 2. Subdomain Exposure
Public subdomains may reveal internal services.

### 3. Employee Information Exposure
Public employee data can be used for social engineering attacks.

## Risk Level
Medium

## Recommendations
- Use role-based email addresses
- Limit exposure of employee details
- Secure and monitor subdomains
