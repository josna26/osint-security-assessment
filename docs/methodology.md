# Methodology

This project follows a structured OSINT investigation approach.

## Phase 1 – Target Definition
A simulated FinTech organization (AstraFin Digital Payments Pvt Ltd) was defined along with its digital assets.

## Phase 2 – Data Modeling
Basic entities such as domain, subdomains, email addresses, and employee identities were created.

## Phase 3 – Relationship Mapping
Using Maltego, relationships between entities were visually represented to simulate an OSINT investigation graph.

## Phase 4 – Exposure Analysis
Initial analysis was performed to identify potential risks such as predictable email patterns and exposed subdomains.

## Phase 5 – Risk Assessment
Basic risks were identified based on the modeled data.
